Introduction

At NEXORA Technologies, Inc. ("NEXORA," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information (PHI). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our MediTwin Bio-Patch device and cloud-based Digital Twin platform (collectively, the "Services").

This policy applies to all users of our Services, including patients, healthcare providers, and enterprise partners. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Key Principle

Your health data belongs to you. We act as stewards of your information, implementing industry-leading security measures and never selling your personal health information to third parties.

Information We Collect

1. Health Data from Bio-Patch Device

Our wearable IoT Bio-Patch continuously monitors and collects the following physiological data:

Cardiovascular Metrics

  • • Heart rate (BPM) - continuous monitoring
  • • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • • Single-lead ECG waveforms (500Hz sampling)

Other Vital Signs

  • • Core body temperature
  • • Galvanic skin response (stress levels)
  • • Motion and activity data (fall detection)
  • • Sleep patterns and quality metrics

2. Account and Profile Information

  • Personal identifiers: Full name, date of birth, gender, contact information
  • Account credentials: Email address, encrypted password
  • Medical history: Pre-existing conditions, medications, allergies (if provided)
  • Emergency contacts: Names and contact details of designated persons

3. Device and Technical Information

  • Device serial number and model (ESP32 hardware ID)
  • Firmware version and software updates
  • Connection logs and data transmission timestamps
  • IP address and network information

4. Usage and Analytics Data

  • Dashboard interactions and feature usage
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Customer support interactions and feedback

How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

1. Health Monitoring and Predictive Analytics

  • • Creating your personalized Digital Twin health model
  • • Real-time vital sign monitoring and anomaly detection
  • • 48-72 hour health trajectory forecasting using LSTM neural networks
  • • Arrhythmia detection through CNN analysis of ECG data
  • • Fall detection and emergency alert systems

2. Service Delivery and Improvement

  • • Maintaining and improving platform functionality
  • • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • • Conducting research to enhance AI model accuracy
  • • Developing new features and health insights

3. Clinical Integration and Care Coordination

  • • Sharing data with authorized healthcare providers (with your explicit consent)
  • • Facilitating telemedicine consultations
  • • Supporting clinical decision-making
  • • Emergency medical response coordination

4. Legal and Compliance

  • • Complying with applicable healthcare regulations (HIPAA, GDPR)
  • • Responding to legal requests and preventing fraud
  • • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • • Protecting rights, property, and safety

Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security controls across all layers of our platform:

Hardware Security

On-chip AES-256 encryption, secure I2C protocols, tamper-resistant casing

Transport Security

TLS 1.3 encryption, secure WebSocket connections, certificate pinning

Cloud Security

Encrypted storage, role-based access control, SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure

Additional Security Practices

  • ✓ Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • ✓ Employee security training and background checks
  • ✓ Multi-factor authentication for all accounts
  • ✓ Automatic session timeout and device management
  • ✓ Comprehensive audit logging and monitoring
  • ✓ Incident response and breach notification protocols

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Never Sell Your Health Data

NEXORA will never sell, rent, or lease your personal health information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

With Your Healthcare Providers

When you explicitly authorize us to share data with your doctors, clinics, or hospitals for treatment purposes.

Service Providers and Partners

With vetted third-party vendors who assist with cloud hosting, data analytics, and platform maintenance under strict confidentiality agreements.

Research and Development (Anonymized)

Aggregated, de-identified data may be used for AI model training and medical research. All personal identifiers are removed using industry-standard anonymization techniques.

Emergency Situations

Critical health alerts may be shared with emergency services and designated emergency contacts to protect your health and safety.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect our legal rights.

Your Privacy Rights

Under HIPAA, GDPR, and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal health information we hold about you in a portable format.

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information in your profile.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your account and associated health data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data while maintaining your account.

Right to Data Portability

Export your health data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

Right to Object

Opt-out of certain data processing activities, including marketing communications and non-essential analytics.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Revoke previously granted permissions for data sharing at any time.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@nexora.tech or submit a request through your account dashboard. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account closure.
  • Health Records: Maintained for 7 years post-closure in compliance with medical record retention laws.
  • De-identified Data: May be retained indefinitely for research and AI model improvement.
  • Backup Systems: Data in backups is automatically purged within 60 days.

HIPAA Compliance

As a healthcare technology provider handling Protected Health Information (PHI), NEXORA complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA):

Administrative Safeguards

  • • Security risk assessments
  • • Workforce training programs
  • • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
  • • Incident response procedures

Physical Safeguards

  • • Facility access controls
  • • Workstation security
  • • Device and media controls
  • • Secure data center operations

Technical Safeguards

  • • Access control mechanisms
  • • Audit controls and logging
  • • Integrity controls
  • • Transmission security (TLS 1.3)

Breach Notification

  • • 60-day notification to affected individuals
  • • HHS reporting for breaches >500 persons
  • • Media notification when required
  • • Detailed breach documentation

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on: (1) Your consent, (2) Contract performance, (3) Legal obligations, and (4) Legitimate interests in providing and improving our Services.

Data Localization

European user data is processed and stored within the EEA. International transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

Data Protection Officer

Contact our EU Data Protection Officer at dpo@nexora.eu

Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not complied with GDPR requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent.

For users aged 13-17, we require parental or legal guardian consent before creating an account. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@nexora.tech.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect:

  • Changes in our Services or business practices
  • New regulatory requirements
  • Technological advancements
  • User feedback and best practices

We will notify you of material changes via: Email to your registered address, in-app notification, and/or prominent notice on our dashboard. Continued use of our Services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes affecting your rights, we will seek your explicit consent.
