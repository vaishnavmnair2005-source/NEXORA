Inside MediTwin

At NEXORA, we don't just track data; we simulate health. Discover the engineering, AI models, and mission that drive the world's most advanced personal digital twin.

The Problem

Traditional healthcare is reactive. We wait for symptoms to appear before seeking help, often when the damage is already done. Latency in detection is the leading cause of chronic complications.

The Solution

MediTwin makes healthcare proactive. By creating a continuous data loop between your body and our AI, we catch anomalies in the sub-clinical phase—days before symptoms even manifest.

Eliminating Latency

  • No more waiting for appointments.
  • No more missing silent irregular heartbeats.
  • Instant data-driven peace of mind.

About The Project

MediTwin is an intelligent healthcare ecosystem. It continuously monitors vital health parameters using our proprietary Wearable IoT Bio-Patch. The system creates a personalized Digital Twin of the user's body in the cloud and uses Deep Learning to predict potential health risks before emergencies occur.

The Neural Backbone

MediTwin utilizes a multi-layered AI architecture to interpret human biology.

CNN Analyzers

Convolutional Neural Networks identify abnormal R-peaks in ECG data, detecting arrhythmias faster than traditional software.

TinyML (On-Edge)

Lightweight Random Forest models run directly on the ESP32 chip to provide offline fall detection and critical spike alerts.

LSTM Forecasts

Long Short-Term Memory networks track historical vitals to forecast your "Health Trajectory" for the next 48-72 hours.

How It Works: The Loop

Stage 01 // Sensing

IoT Bio-Patch

Equipped with MAX30102 and AD8232 sensors, the patch monitors SpO2 and Heart activity. Data is filtered using Python-based DSP algorithms to remove muscle noise and interference.
