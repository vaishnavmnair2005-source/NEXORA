User Security Guidelines

Recommended Practices

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) immediately
  • Use unique passwords (min 12 characters, symbols)
  • Regularly review authorized devices in settings
  • Keep security alerts enabled for login attempts
  • Log out from shared devices after every session

Critical Warnings

  • Never share your password or OTP with anyone
  • Ignore emails asking for passwords (Phishing)
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for checking health data
  • Report unrecognized dashboard activity instantly
  • Do not physically tamper with Bio-Patch hardware
